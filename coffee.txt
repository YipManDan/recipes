Ingredients:
Water
Coffee Beans

1. Grind coffee beans
2. Place coffee grounds at the bottom of a french press.
3. Take boiling water and fill french press to just over the coffee grounds.
4. Gently stir the coffee grounds for about 30 seconds.
5. Pour the rest of the water over the coffee grounds then place the lid on (do not press down on plunger). Steep for 4 minutes.
6. Gently push down on the plunger, making sure to keep your hand away from the pour spout.
7. Pour coffee and enjoy.
